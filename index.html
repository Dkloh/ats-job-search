  • Exact phrases: "junior developer"
  • OR logic: react OR nextjs OR vue
  • Stack combos: typescript tailwind postgres
  • Role variants: "web developer" OR "frontend engineer"

location

  • Remote only: Remote
  • Hybrid: Remote OR Hybrid
  • NYC area: "New York" OR NYC OR Remote
  • WFH: Remote OR WFH OR "work from home"

date_filter

  • Google's after: is approximate — not always reliable
  • After searching, use Google's "Past week" or "Past month" filter for fresher results
  • Set to 2–4 weeks ago for best balance of recency and volume

company_pages

  • Catches company.com/jobs, jobs.company.com, company.com/careers
  • Target startups: add site:.io to keywords
  • Target AI companies: add site:.ai
  • Add industry: fintech OR proptech OR "b2b saas"
